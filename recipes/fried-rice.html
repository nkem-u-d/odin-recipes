fried-rice Recipes

DESCRIPTION

Fried rice is made by stir-frying cooked rice with vegetables, seasonings, and your choice of protein. Everything is tossed in a hot pan or wok to blend flavors. It’s finished with soy sauce, spices, and sometimes scrambled eggs.

INgredients

  • cooked rice
  • mixed vegetables
  • soy Sauce
  • optional protein
  • Oil
  • Egg
  • salt

Steps

  1. Heat oil and sauté onions, garlic, and vegetables.
  2. Push aside and scramble eggs in the same pan..
  3. Add cooked rice and stir-fry everything together.
  4. RSeason with soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  5. Toss well, add green onions, and serve.
