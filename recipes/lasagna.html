lasagna Recipes

DESCRIPTION

Lasagna is made by layering cooked lasagna sheets with a rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta, and melted cheese. The layers are repeated until the dish is full, then topped with cheese. It’s baked until bubbling, golden, and fully set.

INgredients

  • lasagna Noodles
  • Ground beef/ mixed beef with sausage
  • TOmato Sauce
  • onion and Garlic
  • OLive Oil
  • cheese
  • Egg
  • salt

Steps

  1. Cook noodles and prepare the meat sauce
  2. Mix ricotta with seasoning (or make béchamel).
  3. Layer sauce, noodles, and cheese in the dish.
  4. Repeat layers and top with extra cheese
  5. Bake until bubbly, then let it cool before cutting
